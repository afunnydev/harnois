Smart alternative to storage containers 
 
The EzKit™ is a smart alternative to a storage container. One EzKit™ (depending on the model) has the surface storage capacity of up to 13 standard containers of 20’ and has a vertical clearance twice as big. This spacious design allows users to store their goods and even install workstations.
